Fake News Detection

In this project I built two LSTM Neural Networks to perform supervised classification in order to sort out unreliable and reliable news. The first LSTM-NN is based only on the text of the analyzed articles, whereas the second Neural Network classifies news exclusively by means of their titles. While the former obviously achieved a higher accuracy, the latter, relying solely on the title, still provided comparably good results. The dataset (from the Fake News Detection competition on Kaggle) contains the title, author, text, id and an assigned binary label (equal to 1 in case of unreliable news or 0 for reliable news) of 20800 articles.
