IMDB Reviews Classifier

The main purpose of this project was to explore the potentialities of transfer learning. In order to do so, I retrieved the IMDB movie reviews dataset available in TensorFlow Datasets and then, on top of the TF2 Saved Model 'nnlm-en-dim128' (a token based text embedding trained on English Google News 200B corpus and provided by TensorFlow Hub) I trained a simple Neural Network to classify positive (label = 1) and negative (label = 0) movie reviews, thus quickly and effortlessly achieving a reasonable accuracy.
